BattleCON Replay Uploader

To upload your replays:

  • Windows:
    1. Find your .bcr files in %AppData%\Local\BCO_C (or %AppData%\Local\bco_c).
    2. Zip up all your .bcr files.
  • Mac OSX:
    1. Find your .bcr files in $HOME/Library/Application Support/com.level99games.battlecon-mac/
    2. Select all the relevant ones, right click, and Compress.
  • Linux:
    1. Open a Terminal and run the following command, which should create $HOME/Documents/datums.zip: 
      cd ~/Documents; mkdir datums; find ~/.config -name "*.bcr" -exec cp -p {} datums\;; zip -r datums.zip datums;
Click "Choose File" and select the zip file you created, and then click "Upload Replays".
