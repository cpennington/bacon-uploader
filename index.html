BattleCON Replay Uploader

To upload your replays, click "Choose File", and navigate to:

  • Windows: 
    %AppData%\Local\BCO_C
    or
    %AppData%\Local\bco_c
  • Mac: 
    $HOME/Library/Application Support/com.level99games.battlecon-mac/
  • Linux: 
    ~/.config/bco_c/
Select all .bcr files, and then click "Upload Replays".
